#### 🧬 Computational Drug Discovery
**DrugSynth AI / MitoCoreX** — Governed 67-agent AI platform targeting genetically defined mitochondrial disease. First-in-class designs for PINK1 G309D, Keap1, DRP1, NDUFV1, SDHA. 536 tests, 3 campaigns, 11-stage pipeline.

**GeneVector Track** — Four-paper thesis: TCDR standard, TCDR Engine, MitoCoreX case series, Evidence Readiness Index.

#### 🔬 Evidence Synthesis & Genomic Epidemiology
